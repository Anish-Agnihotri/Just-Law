On April 3, 2017, I directed the Registrar to give notice to the Plaintiffs by way of Form

2.1A that the Court was considering making an order under sub-rule 2.1.01 of the Ontario Rulesof Civil Procedure R.R.O. 1990, Reg. 1990, dismissing this action. No written submissions havebeen received by the Plaintiffs within the 15 day period prescribed in Rule 2.1.01(3).

[2]

I accordingly dismiss this action as being frivolous, vexatious and an abuse of the process

of the Court. I further direct the Registrar to provide a copy of this endorsement by mail to thePlaintiff and the Defendant.
